What are the benefits of using aluminum tubing in manufacturing?

Aluminum tubing offers excellent strength-to-weight ratio, corrosion resistance, and versatility in various applications.

How is aluminum tubing produced?

Aluminum tubing is produced through extrusion processes where aluminum is heated and forced through a die to create the desired shape.

What are the common applications of aluminum tubing?

Common applications include automotive parts, aerospace components, construction materials, and HVAC systems.
